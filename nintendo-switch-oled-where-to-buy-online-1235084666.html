Nintendo Switch OLED Is Marked Down to the Cheapest Price Weve Ever Seen

The gaming console is on sale for nearly 25% off its retail price.

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

Since its release in 2021, the Nintendo Switch OLED has been notoriously difficult for gamers to get their hands on, but Walmart has finally re-stocked the gaming console.

The Nintendo Switch OLED (Japanese version) with white Joy-Con controllers is on sale for at $269.99, or nearly 25% off its retail price, at Walmart. This is the gaming console’s all-time lowest price ever.

The new version of the immensely popular Nintendo Switch OLED and it boasts an impressive and sharp seven-inch OLED touchscreen display with a video resolution of Full HD at 1080p. This is a sizable upgrade from the smaller and lightweight Nintendo Switch Lite (bundled with “Animal Crossing: New Horizons” for $199.99 at Target), as well as the original Nintendo Switch model — which is also on sale for $268.89 (re. $299) at Walmart.
