JoJo Siwa Challenges Amanda Kloots to Try Indoor Skydiving For The First Time

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Photo by Steven Simione/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

JoJo Siwa has just taken Amanda Kloots into a flying adventure. The Fit For Christmas star recently accepted the challenge to do indoor skydiving with the help of her fellow Dancing With The Stars alum.

JoJo Siwa, Amanda Kloots Explore Indoor Skydiving

During a recent episode of The Talk, Kloots shared her first time doing indoor skydiving. The Dick Van Dyke 98 Years of Magic star did the challenge alongside Siwa, who has been making the activity for the past years.

The two headed to iFly Indoor Skydiving in Hollywood for the memorable experience. Before Kloots tried the activity, Siwa assured her that she’ll be just fine, only that she’ll feel “some weird muscles” while inside.

“Being a dancer, you have an advantage,” Siwa said. “You’re going to love it. And then you’ll be like, ‘What is this muscle that I have?'”

Siwa then proceeded to give Kloots some advice.

“First time flier, remember the hand signals, leg bent, relax. If there’s a problem, thumbs down and they’ll help you get out. Smile. Sometimes, people forget to smile on their first time.”

Siwa is clearly not unfamiliar to indoor skydiving, trying the sport since she was 15. She, however, took a break from it after a year. She came back to explore the the sport once more and learn more from it six months ago.

After Siwa comfortably seized her turn, excited Kloots then shoot her shot. Fortunately, the experience turned out incredible for the Dick Van Dyke star.

“That was amazing. I feel so weird. I feel so light, I mean you really do feel like you’re flying,” she said.

Kloots Talks Possibility of Dating

Kloots is obviously up for trying new things this year, and apparently, that may also include new romance. Speaking to ET, the tv host talks the possibility of finding love again.

“I know you just spoke about dating again and your dating life on The Talk so I’m curious. What does this next era look like for Amanda Kloots?” Directo, ET’s reporter, asked her.

“Oh, this is like my Taylor Swift Era?” Kloots said. “I don’t know, is it… I don’t know — tall dark and handsome era?”

“You’re trying to find your Travis Kelce?” Directo responded.

“Yeah, it’s my Travis Kelce era,” she said.

Kloots’ husband, Nick Cordero, died in July 2020 due to COVID-19 after a three-month hospitalization. He was 41 years old.
