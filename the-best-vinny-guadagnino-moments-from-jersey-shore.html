The Best Vinny Guadagnino Moments From 'Jersey Shore'

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Photo by Denise Truscello/Getty Images for Chippendales

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Vinny Guadagnino is among the celebrities set to compete in Dancing With The Stars Season 31. Best known as a Jersey Shore star, let’s take a trip down memory lane and revisit Vinny’s best moments in Seaside Heights.

1. When Vinny Guadagnino Does Many Impressions

This Jersey Shore star gave nothing but piece of fun in the popular reality show. When Vinny does impressions, everyone bursts out in laughter. He had us dying of laughter when he attempted to copy everyone in the vacation home.

2. When He Saved Pauly D Off the Duck Phone

No viewer of Jersey Shore can recall Vinny’s impersonation of Mike without laughing. In the early seasons of Jersey Shore, roommates could only communicate with the outside world through a landline, widely known as the duck phone. Vinny successfully saved Pauly D for a good moment when he impersonated Mike as he talked to Pauly D’s stalker.

3. When He and Pauly D Pranked Deena

Vinny and Pauly D are definitely sidekicks in the aspect of pranking their roommates. Deena became a victim when the two swapped her sentimental pictures for their recreation of the same. Understandably, Deena was pissed.

4. When The MVP Spotted a “Fake Ronnie” and Took Him Home

Jersey Shore birthed the “MVP” trio consisting of Mike, Vinny, and Pauly D. Throughout the seasons, the three have bonded amazingly and shared memorable experiences. One of the most unforgettable was probably when they met “Fake Ronnie” who basically has the same physique—and dance moves­ —as Ronnie.

5. When Vinny Ate Pizza in the Strangest Way

Vinny shocked Jersey Shore fans when he finally revealed how he can eat pizza and stick to his strict keto diet. In one episode of the show, he picked just pepperoni and cheese out of six slices of pizza and dumped the crust with no mercy.

6. When Vinny and Pauly D Flipped the House Inside Out

By Season 5, viewers are already aware what Vinny and Pauly D’s bromance could do. When half of the house decided to go camping, the two were enthusiastic enough to unleash their inner interior designers.

7. When G.T.L. Became a Routine

G.T.L. is one of the acronyms that Jersey Shore has popularized. After night outs, men employ the “gym, tan, laundry” routine to sober themselves up. Essentially, this ritual was initiated by men who are fixated in attracting women. For a more profound explanation, Vinny took the viewer to the official G.T.L. tour.
