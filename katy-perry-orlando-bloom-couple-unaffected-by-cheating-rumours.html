Couple unaffected by cheating rumours

The picture itself is not one of those wherein there is an evident display of affection or romance. Rather, it is one which shows the couple in their dressing gowns, lying comically on steps leading up to a building which looks like a hotel.

The 'Roar' singer captioned the picture ‘we cannes’t’  and has been interpreted as her way of letting the public know that the Selena Gomez cheating rumours have had nothing on her relationship with Orlando Bloom.
