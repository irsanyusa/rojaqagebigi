Tetris 30th Anniversary: Man Creates Fully Playable Tetris Shirt

July 3, 2014 11:17 AM EDT

You’ll never be bored again, because now there’s a shirt with a fully-functional Tetris game on it. Yup, this means you can play the classic video game right on your very own abdomen.

YouTube user Marc Kerger crafted the shirt, which features 128 LEDs and an Arduino Uno microcontroller board, in honor of Tetris’s 30th anniversary. All that’s required to play are two AA batteries.

Finally, something you can wear to complement your Tetris-playing tie.
