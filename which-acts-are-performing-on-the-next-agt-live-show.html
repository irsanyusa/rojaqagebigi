America’s Got Talent Season 19 will return on Tuesday night for the second week of live shows. The list of acts who will be performing in the episode has been released, and it features three Golden Buzzer recipients.

Which Acts Are Performing on AGT This Week?

Tuesday’s new episode will feature performances from three acts who earned a Golden Buzzer in the audition round. That includes both of Sofia Vergara’s Golden Buzzers — Malambo group Legión and Japanese comedy duo Schumacher.

Drone group Sky Elements, who earned the Golden Buzzer from Simon Cowell, will also perform on Tuesday’s show. The episode will also feature singers Alex Sampson and Brooke Bailey, dance group AIRFOOTWORKS, and solo dancer Bboy Samuka.

Acrobat Ashlee Montague, band Biko’s Manna, musician NiNi, and magician Young-Min will also take the stage on Tuesday night for their chance to earn America’s vote and move on to the Season 19 Semifinals — or possibly earn the Golden Buzzer from one of the judges to move straight to the Finals.

Which Acts Moved on Last Week?

Last Tuesday marked the first live show of the season, as the first 11 acts took the stage. Sofia Vergara hit the Golden Buzzer for Hakuna Matata Acrobats, sending them straight to the Finals. America voted for three other acts to move on.

Those acts were Golden Buzzer singer Richard Goodall, country trio Ashes & Arrows, and dog act Roni & Rhythm. They’ll return to perform in the Semifinals later this season for their last chance to make it into the finale.
