Most U.S. Consumers Dont Know What Carbon-Neutral' Means

May 27, 2024 As the world runs out of time to prevent the worst effects of climate change, brands have come under pressure to reduce the pollution generated by their operations. Some, including major names like Mondelēz International Inc. and Mars Inc., have designated their products as “carbon neutral” to signal a sense of environmental responsibility. The label indicates that, whatever emissions were released in the creation of the product, the brand compensated for their harmful effects by purchasing “offsets,” or donations to projects that reduce emissions  such as by planting trees or sequestering carbon.
