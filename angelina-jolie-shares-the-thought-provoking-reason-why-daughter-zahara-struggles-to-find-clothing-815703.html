Angelina Jolie Shares the Thought-Provoking Reason Why Daughter Zahara Struggles to Find Clothing

Angelina Jolie got candid about how her 18-year-old daughter has been having issues when it comes to shopping for new clothes. The veteran actress explained why and revealed how her daughter’s struggles inspired her to create her own fashion brand. And make sure to keep reading until the end because we have a few related bonus tips prepared for you!

The actress only realized her daughter’s issues after they went shopping together.

Invision/Invision/East News

Angelina Jolie doesn’t often give extended interviews, but when she does, she seems to make sure she’s as frank as it gets. In a September 2023 interview with Vogue magazine, the 48-year-old actress opened up about all things relating to her new own fashion brand, Atelier Jolie, including what motivated her to venture professionally into the fashion industry. Given she’s a very family-oriented person, it’s no wonder her daughter’s struggles moved her to the business.

According to Jolie, her daughter Zahara, whom she adopted at 6-months-old from an orphanage in Ethiopia on July 6, 2005, doesn’t have an easy time finding clothing that suits her skin tone. If you’re thinking this issue is something that you’ve never thought about before, you’re not alone. Jolie herself admitted that.

“Obviously, as a white woman, I’ve never had this experience,” Jolie said. “It never crossed my mind until we went shopping together, and I saw that there’s so much room for improvement.”

Jolie involved her children in the launch of her fashion studio.

With her children’s issues at heart, Jolie made sure to include them in the process of creating Atelier Jolie. Zahara herself and Pax, her 19-year-old son whom she adopted from an orphanage in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam on March 15, 2007, were “heavily involved” in the launch. However, the actress finds a certain irony in the fact that they’re now linked to the world of fashion as a family, given neither has never been particularly focused on it.

“I find it slightly funny that we are involved in fashion — I don’t think any one of us is overly ’fashionable,’” Jolie revealed, jokingly. “But because we live in our clothes, it is so much a part of who we are, and something that’s important to explore, especially for young people.”

Color is key when it comes to fashion, according to an expert.

Stylist Cristina Ehrlich, who works with celebrities such as Laura Dern and Natasha Lyonne, emphasized in an interview the importance of finding what hue best suits your skin tone. “Color is the number one most important thing to me when it comes to styling; it’s what makes my service unique,” she revealed. “The first thing I do with a new client is study their skin tone and figure out what’s going to look best. It’s easy to wear black, but color can be so beautiful.”

With that in mind, we gathered a few tips from different fashion experts to help you decide what you should wear next. But remember: feeling comfortable in what you wear is the most important!

Find what colors suit your skin tone the best:

So, those with a warm skin tone have several key characteristics that define their color palette and preferences. Firstly, their skin tone likely features a greenish or yellowish undertone, and they may even have an olive complexion. This warm undertone extends to their hair color, which typically falls between dark brown and dark blonde. Additionally, they have a preference for yellow gold jewelry, as it complements their skin tone beautifully.

When it comes to colors that suit them best, warm skin tones harmonize with a spectrum of shades. Warm skin tones are often connected to colors such as orange, yellow, and bright hues. Plus, natural earth tones such as browns, deep tonal reds, and rich greens are also flattering choices. While there aren’t strict rules about colors to avoid, black should only be worn with specific pieces.

Celebrities like Beyoncé, Jennifer Lopez, and Jessica Alba are excellent sources of style inspiration for those with warm skin tones.

People in the cool-toned group can have a skin tone that’s either very fair to very dark, however they usually feature bluish undertones. Their hair color typically ranges from very dark, including blue-black or deep brown shades, to light blonde. Jewelry-wise, they often lean towards silver, rose gold, or a combination of both.

When selecting colors that complement cooler skin tones, charcoal, cool blues, and violet are excellent choices. However, experts advise that it’s essential to be mindful of strong, bold colors, as they can make cool skin tones appear paler. When it comes to colors to avoid, it might be wise to steer clear of overly bold choices like electric blue or grassy green, oranges and yellows.

For style inspiration, you can look to celebrities like Viola Davis, Nicole Kidman, Lupita Nyong’o and Cate Blanchett, who all showcase cool-toned elegance.

A neutral skin tone is one that is challenging to categorize as either warm or cool. Similarly, those with this type of skin ton often have a hair color that tends to be a blend of different shades, such as blonde with ashy streaks or brown with warmer tones. When it comes to jewelry, they have a versatile approach and enjoy mixing and matching various metals.

When selecting colors that enhance your neutral skin tone, it’s best to stick with shades that fall in the middle of the color spectrum. However, if you want to add some flair to your wardrobe beyond white or beige, experts suggest exploring colors like light peach, dusty pinks, soft rose, peony pink, placid blue, jade green, and cameo green. On the flip side, stay away from colors like reds, yellows, and overly vibrant hues. If you do go for yellow, it’s advisable to select a softer version like cornsilk or buff.

Celebrities like Selena Gomez, Kerry Washington, Angelina Jolie, and Julia Roberts can be your source of style inspiration, as they showcase the versatility of neutral skin tones.
