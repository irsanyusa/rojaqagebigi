For Natural Sisters: Lady Resorts to Braiding Own Hair After Stressful Methods at Salons

  • A TikToker identified on the platform as @anointingea has shared how she braided her hair
  • In the clip, she did a voiceover stating reasons she doesn't let stylists at salons weave her hair
  • The video has since gone viral with over 600,000 views on the platform, as many people agreed with her reasons

Natural hair sisters often have a tough time whenever they go to salons to braid their hair, and this has to do with the hair texture and a stylist's inability to work with it.

Most ladies with natural hair who get braids done at salons complain about having to blow-dry the hair and the stylist's insistence on using gel to give a sleek parting.

Well, TikTok user, @anointingea, seems to have had enough and has taken matters into her own hands - literally.
