The best accommodation options for international students in Czechia - Live &amp; Study

6. 6. 2023 | Student Life

Finding a suitable and affordable place to live is one of the most important tasks for international students studying in the Czech Republic. With a wide range of accommodation options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we’ll explore the best accommodation options for international students in the Czech Republic.

  • Dormitories

    • Dormitories, also known as student residences, are the most popular choice for international students in the Czech Republic. They offer affordable accommodation, a great community atmosphere, and the opportunity to meet other students from all over the world. Some of the dormitories are run by universities while others are managed by private companies. The facilities in dormitories vary from basic to modern, but they typically include shared kitchens, laundry rooms, study rooms, and social areas.

  • Private Apartments

    • Private apartments are another popular option for international students. Renting a private apartment gives students more privacy and independence than dormitories. Private apartments in the Czech Republic can be found through real estate agencies or online platforms such as Bezrealitky, Flatio, or Sreality. Prices vary depending on location and the size of the apartment, but they are generally more expensive than dormitories.

  • Homestays

    • Homestays are a great option for students who want to experience local culture and improve their language skills. Living with a local family allows students to immerse themselves in the Czech way of life and practice their language skills in a natural setting. Homestays are typically arranged through agencies, and the costs vary depending on the location and the services provided.

  • Flatshares

    • Flatshares, also known as shared apartments, are a popular option for students who want to live with other students or young professionals. Flatshares in the Czech Republic can be found through online platforms such as Sreality, Bezrealitky, or Facebook groups. Living in a flatshare is a great way to save money on rent, share living expenses, and meet new people.

  • Hostels

    • Hostels are a great option for students who are on a tight budget or who are looking for short-term accommodation. Hostels in the Czech Republic offer basic facilities such as shared dormitories, private rooms, and shared bathrooms. They are generally cheaper than other types of accommodation, but they are not as comfortable or convenient as other options.

  • Hotels

    • Hotels are an option for students who are looking for more luxurious accommodation or who are only staying for a short period. Hotels in the Czech Republic vary in price and quality, but they generally offer more privacy and comfort than other types of accommodation.

    In conclusion, the best accommodation option for international students in the Czech Republic depends on their preferences, budget, and length of stay. Dormitories are the most popular and affordable option, while private apartments offer more privacy and independence. Homestays are great for cultural immersion, while flatshares offer the opportunity to live with other students or young professionals. Hostels and hotels are also available for students on a budget or those looking for short-term accommodation.
