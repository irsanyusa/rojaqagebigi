Hot Orange chocolate | Pulse Nigeria

Ingredients

1-1/2 cups whole milk

1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

ADVERTISEMENT

2 teaspoons Truvia sweetener (more if you want it sweeter)

1 teaspoon orange zest

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (orange extract can be used for even more flavor)

Whipped cream or marshmallows, for topping

Directions

ADVERTISEMENT

Add all ingredients except the whipped cream into your blender. If you have Blender that has a hot soup preset option set it and forget it! (Well, don't forget it...drink it after it's done) After your hot chocolate is finished pour it into a mug, top with whipped cream or marshmallows.

If your blender does not have a preset option simple blend on high until hot. This will take about 4 minutes.

* If you don't have a blender you could simply add all ingredients (except marshmallows and whipped cream) to a small saucepan and heat over medium heat until fully melted and combined.
