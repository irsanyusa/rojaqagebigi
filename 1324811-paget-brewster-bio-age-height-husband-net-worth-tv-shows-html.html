Early life and family

Paget Valerie Brewster was born on March 10th 1969 to Hathaway Brewster (nee Tew), a government official, and Galen Brewster, a school administrator. The actress was named after the 1950s actress Debra Paget.

The actress was born in Concord, Massachusetts, the United States of America. She also grew up in Massachusetts.

Paget has a younger brother named Ivan.

For her schooling, Paget attended a New England boarding school where her parents were teachers. She then moved to New York City to attend Parsons School of Design.

She dropped out of the design school during her first year in order to pursue acting. She made her acting debut during her first year at Parsons School of Design.

Paget also studied at the Jean Shelton Acting Studio.

Career

In the mid-1990s, Paget moved to Los Angeles and started taking acting classes. Between 1994 and 1995, she hosted a late-night talk show in the San Francisco Bay Area. The talk show was called The Paget Show, and it had 65 episodes.

The actress is well known for her excellent portrayal of Emily Prentiss in Criminal Minds, an American police procedural crime drama television series. Emily Prentiss first appeared on the show in season 2 episode 9, which was titled 'The Last Word'. Emily replaced Elle Greenaway on the TV show.

Prentiss is an intelligent profiler with unique insight and a way of connecting with victims.

Paget was a regular on the show from season 2 to season 7 (2006 to 2012) when she left to pursue other things. She returned at the end of season 11, during the last episode. She then came back for good in season 12.
